Daniel Zahnd, born in 1995, has been married to Regula for five years. They live with their son in Rüschegg Heubach and are joyfully anticipating the arrival of a new family member.

Daniel enjoys learning, solving problems, and gaining hands-on experience in physics, engineering, and other sciences. As a husband, father, and recently a homeowner, he cherishes spending time with his family and takes pride in maintaining their home. A devout Christian, he is actively and voluntarily involved in a local congregation near his home. As a committed follower of Jesus Christ, he also has a deep personal interest in theology and philosophy.

Professionally, Daniel completed an apprenticeship as an automation technician before pursuing studies in physics, mathematics, and astronomy at the University of Bern. He earned his BSc in 2023 and his MSc in Physics in 2024. Currently, he is pursuing a PhD in Engineering Sciences (2024–2028) at the Swiss Federal Institute of Metrology, in collaboration with the University of Bern.
