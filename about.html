Daniel Zahnd, born in 1995, has been married to Regula for five years. They live with their son in Rüschegg Heubach and are eagerly awaiting the arrival of a new family member who is currently on the way.

Daniel likes learning, solving problems and gaining hands-on experience in physics, engineering and other sciences. As a husband, father and most recently a houseowner, he furthermore loves to spend time with his family and engange in all kinds of work to keep a house in good condition. As a devout christian, he is passionately and voluntarily engaged in a christian congregation near his home. Being a determined follower of Jesus Christ, he has a strong lay interest in theology and philosophical questions.

Professionally, he completed an apprenticeship as an automation technician and went on to study physics, mathematics, and astronomy at the University of Bern, where he earned his BSc in 2023 and MSc in Physics in 2024. He is currently (2024–2028) pursuing a PhD in Engineering Sciences at the Swiss Federal Institute of Metrology in collaboration with the University of Bern.
